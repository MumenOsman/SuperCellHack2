# Game Design Overview

## Core Fantasy
Be a small rabbit exploring a giant world at your own pace, with the game itself learning and adapting to you.

## Core Loop
Move → Jump → Collect carrots → AI learns your skill → Difficulty adjusts → New routes open

## Missions (5-10Min Demo)
- Collect all carrots
- Reach the top counter
- Complete a jump chain without falling
- Explore multiple paths to find your style

## Why Cozy?
- No timers or pressure
- No punishment or fail states
- Soft feedback and bouncy mechanics
- AI adapts to YOU, not the other way around

## Target Audience
Casual players, families, and players who enjoy relaxing but engaging experiences.
